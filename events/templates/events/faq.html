General

EventSphere is a college event management platform that connects students with amazing events happening at colleges across India. Students can discover, register and track events, while professors can create and manage their events easily.

Yes! EventSphere is completely free for both students and professors. Create your account, discover events and register — all at zero cost. No hidden charges, no credit card required.

EventSphere supports colleges across India. Any professor from any college can create an account and start posting events. We're growing every day with new colleges joining our platform.

For Students

Registering is super simple! Just:

  1. Create a free student account
  2. Browse events on the Events page
  3. Click on any event to view details
  4. Click "Register Now" button
  5. Fill your name, email and phone
  6. Done! You'll get a confirmation

Absolutely! You can register for as many events as you want. All your registrations are saved in your "My Tickets" section in the dashboard for easy access.

Don't worry! Our system prevents duplicate registrations. If you try to register for the same event twice, you'll see a message saying "You are already registered" and no duplicate entry will be created.

After logging in, go to your Student Dashboard and click on "My Tickets" in the sidebar. You'll see all your upcoming and past event registrations with your ticket ID.

For Professors

Steps to create an event:

  1. Create a Professor account
  2. Login and go to Dashboard
  3. Click "Add New Event"
  4. Fill all event details
  5. Submit for admin approval
  6. After approval, event goes live!

All events go through an admin review process before going live. This ensures quality and authenticity of events on our platform. Approval usually happens within 24 hours. You'll be able to see the status in your dashboard.

Yes! In your Professor Dashboard, go to "My Events" and click the users icon next to any event. You'll see a full list of registered students with their name, email and phone number. You can also export the data as CSV.
