📖 Our Journey

How We Got Here

From a simple idea to India's growing event platform

2023

The Idea

A group of college students frustrated with missing out on events came up with the idea of a unified platform.

Early 2024

First Version

EventSphere v1.0 launched with basic event listing. 5 colleges and 200 students joined in the first week!

Mid 2024

Growing Fast

Added professor dashboards, registration system and email notifications. Crossed 1,000 users!

2025

Major Upgrade

Complete redesign with premium UI, 50+ colleges onboarded and 10,000+ students registered on the platform.

2026

Today & Beyond

Continuously improving with new features, mobile app coming soon and expanding to more colleges across India.
